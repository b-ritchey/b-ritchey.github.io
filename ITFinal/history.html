Learning about history is one of my favorite hobbies.

Within history, I enjoy studying military history.

Some of my favorite wars to study are both World Wars, The Napoleonic Wars, and The Civil War

Some of my favorite generals are Napoleon, U.S. Grant, and Hannibal of Carthage.

I also enjoy learning about ancient and medieval history. There is so much to to learn from the past to help us as people go forward that I encourage everyone to learn about history to some extent!

One of my favorite youtube channels for history content is Vlogging Through History. I highly recommend you check out his channel!
